# Contributing

This is a documentation and product-evaluation repository. Corrections to public requirements, accessibility text, broken links, and reproducible issues in the sample pack are welcome.

Keep every claim consistent with the screenshots, sample output, and documented product boundary. Do not submit proprietary application source, third-party binaries, customer documents, generated customer outputs, credentials, local filesystem paths, or PDFs without clear redistribution rights.

Open a focused pull request that states what changed, why the public evidence supports it, and how links and archive integrity were checked.
